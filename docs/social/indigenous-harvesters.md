## Facebook

Band office contacts for 637 First Nations communities don't live in one place — they're scattered across ISC government profiles, community websites, and PDFs. I built a two-notebook Python pipeline to collect them: ISC profiles for addresses and phone numbers, community website scraping for Chief/council emails. The data flows into North Cloud for ongoing sync.
