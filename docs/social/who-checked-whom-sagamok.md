## LinkedIn

Who provides independent oversight when administrative and corporate roles overlap in a First Nation's governance?

New video on the RHT Circle channel. Laura Owl joins me to review the public records around Angus Toulouse's roles at Sagamok. The documents identify him as Sagamok's Senior Administrative Officer, show his signature on both sides of the audit responsibility page, and record leadership roles in Nation-owned companies and other organizations.

Every source shown is public: Sagamok annual reports, audited financial statements, a public job posting, Ontario company records.

We walk through what the documents say and ask the structural question: when the same person holds the administration and the companies, who checks whom?
