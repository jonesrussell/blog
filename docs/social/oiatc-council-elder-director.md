## LinkedIn

The Ontario Indigenous AI & Technology Council is growing, and I'm glad to share who's joining.

Steven Bennett joins as our Elder and Knowledge Keeper. Steven is a fluent speaker of Anishinaabemowin whose teachings and recordings are the foundation of our language work. He'll guide the council on language, on protocol, and on how community knowledge is used. I'm grateful he said yes.

Oliver Zielke joins as a director. Oliver is a former Director at Web Networks, our hosting partner, with a background in non-profit and open-source technology.

We're keeping the council small and growing it on fit, not urgency, as we incorporate as a not-for-profit. There's still room: one more arm's-length director, and a first partner Nation.
